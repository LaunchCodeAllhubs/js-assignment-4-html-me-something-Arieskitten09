What I Like To Do In My Free Time

    • Hiking

    The best kind of exploration for me is being physically active. In college, I did my best to go on every trip available to me because like traveling in general, I love seeing and experiencing new things. I may not be able to say I climbed a cone volcano like my brother (there was an attempt), I've climbed in and out of a gorge with a in North Carolina. Went searching for fossilized teeth in the Mississippi gullies (and nearly lost a shoe). The best by far was going 300 feet into one of the longest cave systems on the continent to see an historical sinkhole in person.

    Playing video games

    I've beem playing video games since I was 4 years old. My first system was the Super Nintendo but I also played on my Mom's Sega. Outside of Atari, I do believe I've played on every system after its creation...well except for GameBoys until the SP version. Either Killer Instinct or Street Fighter were the first games I played, but the PageMaster music still plays in my head. My dexterity was built for computers!

    Photography

    According to my baby pictures, I've been best friends with cameras since a young age. From diapers until now, I've either been in front of a camera or behind it though my preference between the two have changed . I prefer to take pictures of nature and of unobstructed infastructures. I especially love sky pics. DSLR cameras are another addition to the friendship club.

    Watching anime

    In my younger days, I dabbled in anime. The first show I remember was Dragon Ball and I was only enamored because Goku had a tail. I was reintroduced to it in community college through a friend who had me spend the night so we could stay up and watch Princess Mononoke. Since then...binging became a habit and I could easily watch 10-20 episodes of any show that caught my interest. I've watched every episode of Dragon Ball that I know of until Dragon Ball GT, every episode of Naruto including the movies, Dororo is still my favorite and I love nearly any Miyazaki film. I will not say I'm anywhere near a master of anime, but my watching and reading list is extensive.
